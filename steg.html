Image Steganography

Hide text inside other images...

This is a client-side Javascript tool to steganographically hide plaintext inside the two lower bits of image's pixels. The program correctly hides the first 255 characters in the ASCII table and only works well on devices with larger viewport. May produce wrong results if image passed is transparent. Select whether to hide or show the text below. The images are processed as being (300px by 300px), thus a total of 90000 - 1 characters can be hidden per each image.
